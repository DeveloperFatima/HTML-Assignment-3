About Us:

WE USE FRESH INGREDIENTS. WHY IS THAT?

A pizza made with ingredients from cans and store shelves doesn't belong on your plate. With fresh pizza, your senses are overwhelmed
with flavors you've never experienced. You can even see the difference when you look at fresh pizza. The vegetables are vibrant,
the meats are juicy, and the crust is light and airy.

When it comes to making a delicious pizza, the quality of the ingredients you use can make all the difference. Fresh, high-quality
ingredients not only taste better, but they also offer a number of other benefits that make them worth the extra effort.

One of the biggest benefits of using fresh, high-quality ingredients is the flavor. Fresh vegetables and meats have a bold, natural
flavor that simply can’t be matched by processed or frozen options. At T minus 90 Pizzeria, we also use high-quality D.O.P ingredients
for most of our toppings, which adds an extra level of authenticity and flavor to our pizzas. This means that your pizzas will taste better
and have a more complex and satisfying flavor.

In addition to the flavor, using fresh, high-quality ingredients is also better for your health. Fresh vegetables and meats are generally more
nutrient-rich than their processed counterparts, which means that your pizzas will be a healthier option. This is especially important if you’re
making pizzas for children or for people with dietary restrictions.

At T minus 90 Pizzeria in Hughesdale, Melbourne, we believe that using the freshest and highest-quality ingredients is the key to creating
delicious pizzas. From our locally-sourced vegetables to our hand-crafted dough made with only the finest flours, we go above and beyond to ensure
that every pizza we serve is of the highest quality. Come taste the difference for yourself!
